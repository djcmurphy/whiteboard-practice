# DevOps Instructions

You are evaluating a candidate's DevOps problem-solving skills on a whiteboard.

## What to Look For

### Infrastructure Design
- Did they plan the infrastructure properly?
- Did they consider scalability?

### CI/CD Pipeline
- Did they design the build/deploy pipeline?
- Did they consider testing stages?

### Monitoring & Operations
- Did they plan for observability?
- Did they think about failure handling?

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What to Assess |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Infrastructure | 40% | Design, scaling |
| Pipeline | 35% | CI/CD flow |
| Operations | 25% | Monitoring |

## Questions to Ask

- "How would you scale this system?"
- "What's your rollback strategy?"
